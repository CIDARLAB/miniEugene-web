Orientation Constraints

Orientation Constraints restrict the orientation (forward or reverse) of genetic components.
Constraint Description Examples
ALL_FORWARD all components in the design must have a forward orientation. ALL_FORWARD
ALL_REVERSE all components in the design must have a reverse orientation. ALL_REVERSE
